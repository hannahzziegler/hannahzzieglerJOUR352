Chocolate Peanut Butter Ice Cream

Satisfy your sweet tooth with this delicious and healthy dessert recipe! This chocolate peanut butter ice cream will take less time than driving to your favorite ice cream shop, be half the price and twice as delicious!

This recipe will take 20 minutes to prepare and will make two servings of delicious peanut butter chocolate ice cream.

All you need are the ingredients below and an ice cream maker.

Ingredients

  • 3 oz bittersweet chocolate
  • 6 tablespoons of cocoa powder
  • 2 1/2 cups of milk
  • 3 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup whipping cup cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter (use fresh ground, 100% peanuts peanut butter if possible)

Cooking Preparation

  1. Melt chocolate over low heat
  2. Gradually stir in cocoa powder and milk while continuing to heat
  3. Stir until consistency is thick but not solid
  4. Beat sugar into eggs
  5. Stir the chocolate and milk mixture into eggs
  6. Add cream and vanilla extract
  7. Let cool
  8. Stir cup of ice cream mixture into peanut butter just before adding to ice cream maker
  9. After mixing, stir into the rest of the ice cream mixture
  10. Freeze in ice cream maker according to manufacturer's instructions
